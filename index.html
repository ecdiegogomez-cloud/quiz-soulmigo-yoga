Essentials (everyone needs these)
Yoga Mat
A non-slip mat is essential for safety and comfort. Look for 4-6mm thickness for joint protection.

Materials: PVC (durable, budget-friendly) or natural rubber (eco-friendly, excellent grip).
Textures: Smooth for comfort, or textured for extra grip if you sweat a lot.
Avoid ultra-cheap mats (£10-15) because they wear out quickly and can be slippery. Invest in quality for better grip and durability.
Comfortable Clothing
Leggings or fitted shorts that allow movement without revealing too much.

Materials: Look for stretchy, breathable fabrics like cotton blends or moisture-wicking technical fabrics.
Fit: Fitted enough to see your alignment, but comfortable for all ranges of motion.
Avoid overly baggy clothes because they make it hard to check your form and can get in the way.
